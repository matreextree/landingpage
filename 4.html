The analysts find that globally spending on management consultants has grown to $125.2 billion in 2014, up 6.1% from $118.1 billion in 2013**.

The top four largest consulting firms, all with a heritage in the accountancy sector, known as the Big Four Deloitte, PwC, EY and KPMG), hold a combined 40% of the total consulting market.
